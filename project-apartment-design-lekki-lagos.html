"Lekki rewards architecture that knows its address — this apartment design answers the peninsula's energy with a building that is polished, composed, and unapologetically of its place."

Lekki has become Lagos's most aspirational residential address, and architecture here is expected to carry weight — to signal quality and contemporary thinking in equal measure. This apartment design commission gave MAF Design Projects the opportunity to work within that expectation and exceed it. The brief required a building that would be immediately legible as a premium residential offer while remaining practical and functional for the people who would actually live in it.

The facade response is disciplined and modern: a composition of recessed balconies, horizontal banding, and carefully considered apertures that balance transparency with privacy. The building reads as a coherent object from the street whilst offering each apartment unit genuine privacy from its neighbours. Selected external finishes were chosen for longevity in Lagos's coastal climate — materials that age gracefully rather than deteriorate.

Interior layouts prioritise the quality of the living and sleeping zones, with generous room proportions and ceiling heights that elevate the everyday experience of the space. The project was fully documented in 3D and delivered with a comprehensive set of architectural drawings for the client's use with contractors and planning authorities.
